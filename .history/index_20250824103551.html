About FloodSafe

FloodSafe is an advanced flood risk assessment system that uses AI technology to analyze environmental data and predict potential flood risks.

Our system processes various data inputs including images, water level measurements, and rainfall data to provide accurate risk assessments.

Key Features:

  • Image-based flood detection
  • Real-time risk assessment
  • Historical data analysis
  • Actionable recommendations
